Payment Methods

At SBRE, we understand that flexibility in payment is crucial when investing in Dubai’s dynamic real estate market. That’s why we offer a variety of secure and convenient payment options tailored to both local and international buyers.

Bank Transfer (Local & International)

Secure bank transfers are the most common method for property purchases in Dubai. We accept both local AED transfers and international wire transfers in USD, EUR, GBP, and other major currencies. SWIFT instructions are provided upon request.

Developer Installment Plans

Many properties listed on SBRE come with flexible installment payment plans directly from developers. These typically include:
10-20% down payment
Installments during construction (e.g., 30-50%)
Final payment upon handover
No interest is usually applied, and approval is straightforward.

Mortgage Financing

We collaborate with leading banks in the UAE to offer mortgage options for eligible buyers, including:
Up to 80% financing for UAE residents
Up to 50% financing for non-residents
Tenure up to 25 years
Fixed or variable interest rates
Our consultants will assist you through pre-approval and documentation.

Post-Handover Payment Plans

Some developers offer post-handover plans, allowing you to pay a portion of the property value even after moving in—ideal for buyers seeking cash flow flexibility.

Cash Payments

For select projects and ready properties, cash payments are accepted. A secure transaction is facilitated through escrow accounts regulated by the Dubai Land Department (DLD).

Financing structure: your path to a profitable deal in the UAE

Acquiring a profitable business requires smart financial planning. Our role is to look beyond standard bank loans. We act as your personal CFO, minimizing your upfront investment and structuring the deal so that the business achieves maximum capital return as quickly as possible. We analyze every available instrument—from local bank financing to flexible seller financing schemes.

3 Key Stages of Financial Support

  • Expert Financial Modeling (Cash Flow Projections)

    We conduct an in-depth analysis of the target business’s financial health. Our team models the Cash Flow to determine the company’s realistic capacity to service debt. This allows us to calculate the optimal ratio of equity to debt and present a robust, data-backed financing request to the bank.

  • Access to Local Bank Financing

    Leveraging our long-standing relationships with leading UAE banks (including both Islamic and conventional financial institutions), we significantly simplify the process of securing a business acquisition loan. We pre-package all documentation and act as the intermediary, which helps to expedite approval and negotiate the most favourable lending terms for the investor.

  • Structuring Vendor Financing (Seller Finance)

    In many cases, the most advantageous solution involves flexible payment schemes with seller participation. We professionally structure deferred payments and ’Earn-Out’ agreements (payment of the remainder upon achieving key performance indicators). This reduces your initial financial risk and ensures that the financial interests of both the seller and the buyer remain aligned during the transition period.
